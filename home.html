What You Get

Everything a Grey Nomad Needs

From a weekend run to the full Big Lap — the AI plans it, you drive it.

🤖
AI Route Planning

Powered by Groq's llama-3.3-70b. Enter start, end, and via towns — get a full day-by-day itinerary with camps, fuel stops, and points of interest in seconds.

📍
Via Towns

Route through specific towns in order — Gladstone → Clermont → Townsville — and the AI breaks the route into clean, accurate legs for each segment.

⛽
Fuel Cost Calculator

Import your vehicle from LogMyJourney AU or enter manually. Get total fuel in litres, cost in AUD, and fill-up count — per leg and for the whole trip.

🗺️
Google Maps Links

Every leg opens as a clean A→B Google Maps directions link. Tap on your phone to launch navigation. No encoded POI addresses — just clear driving routes.

▣
QR Codes per Leg

Plan on the laptop at camp, scan the QR with your phone camera, and launch Maps navigation instantly. Save QR codes as PNG for printing.

💬
AI Chat Advisor

Ask anything after planning — fuel gaps on remote roads, dog-friendly camps, road conditions, seasonal timing, Gibb River Road suitability for your rig.

How It Works

Four Steps to the Open Road

1
Get Your API Key

Free Groq account at console.groq.com. No credit card. Takes 60 seconds. Paste it into the app once — it saves locally.

2
Set Your Route

Enter start and end towns. Add via towns for specific routing. Choose your trip length and rig type.

3
Pick Your POIs

Free camps, dump points, national parks, showgrounds, lookouts, roadhouses — tick what matters to you.

4
Hit the Road

Open each leg in Google Maps, scan QR codes, export the itinerary, or keep chatting with the AI advisor.
